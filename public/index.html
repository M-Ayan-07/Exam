How it works

Three steps to a verified exam.

No plugins, no extensions, no hardware. Pure browser-level integrity.

01
🔐

Student Joins

Student logs in and enters the secure exam environment. Monitoring initialises silently in the background.

02
👁

Behaviour + Face Captured

Browser events, camera feed, face presence, gaze direction, and multi-face checks — all timestamped and logged silently.

03
📊

Report Generated

On submission, Gemini AI synthesises all signals into a trust score and human-readable analysis.

Capabilities

Everything it detects.

Browser monitoring + AI face detection + mobile screen monitoring. One credibility score.

browser_01

Tab Switch

Logs every navigation away from the exam tab with frequency and exact timestamps.

browser_02

Window Blur

Tracks focus loss even without a visible tab change — catches split-screen tricks.

browser_03

Clipboard Block

Intercepts all copy/paste events so questions can't be extracted to external tools.

browser_04

DevTools Guard

Detects when browser developer tools are opened during the exam session.

browser_05

Resize Detection

Flags window resizing — the classic side-by-side cheat sheet technique.

browser_06

Shortcut Blocking

Disables view-source, save, print, find and F12 shortcuts that could compromise integrity.

face_ai_01

📷 Face Detection

Uses face-api.js with TinyFaceDetector to verify a single student is present in camera throughout the exam.

face_ai_02

👀 Gaze Tracking

Monitors eye landmarks to detect when a student looks away from the screen for extended periods.

face_ai_03

👥 Multi-Face Alert

Detects multiple faces in the camera frame — flags potential outside assistance instantly.

browser_07

😴 Idle Detection

Flags 60 seconds of inactivity with a toast warning, then logs a violation — catches students who walk away.

mobile_01

📱 Mobile QR Monitor

A QR code on the pre-exam page lets students link their phone as a secondary camera to record the laptop screen — no app needed.

mobile_02

🎬 Defense Video

If the student believes the AI made a mistake, the mobile recording auto-uploads after the exam as a defense video for invigilator review. Deleted after 24h with a tamper-proof audit log.

report_01

📊 Violation Heatmap

The student report shows a minute-by-minute colour-coded heatmap — green clean, yellow low, orange medium, red high — so patterns are visible at a glance.
